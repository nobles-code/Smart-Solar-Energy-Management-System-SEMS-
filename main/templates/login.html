Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect my solar system to the platform?

Connecting your solar system is simple. Once you purchase our smart controller, you'll receive a unique Device ID. Register using this ID, follow the setup instructions in your welcome email, and your system will be connected within minutes.

What information can I monitor with this system?

Our platform provides comprehensive monitoring including real-time power generation, energy consumption, battery status (if applicable), historical performance data, and financial savings. You can also set up custom alerts and notifications.

Is my data secure on your platform?

Absolutely. We implement industry-leading security measures including end-to-end encryption, secure authentication, and regular security audits. Your data is stored securely and never shared with third parties without your explicit consent.

Can I control other smart home devices?

Yes! Our platform integrates with most popular smart home systems including smart thermostats, lighting, and appliances. This allows you to create energy-saving automations based on your solar production.
